Why BushSearch was developed
BushSearch was developed to aid the informal learning of Bushcraft. Bushcraft is the use and practice of skills that allow oneâ€™s survival in a natural environment. The application targets adults in the United Kingdom(UK) interested in learning Bushcraft in their own time outside the formal educational system.

Purpose of the Search tool
The search tool retrieves bushcraft content from keywords typed by the user. For example, a user may type, "How can I survive with a cotton bud in the forest", and then the system will retrieve bushcraft content related to the keyword, cotton bud.

Purpose of the Tool kit
The tool kit is a preloaded object classification. The preloaded objects are survival kit items such as candles and matches, spare batteries, and a first aid kit. The preloaded objects are displayed on the user interface for users (who may not know what to search for) to select the items into the search tool.
